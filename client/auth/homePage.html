After a successful login or account registration, AuthC provides developers a user's access token.


The access token contains information about the user. Decode the token's payload here to find out.

When you're ready, tell AuthC to redirect users after they login to your application's home page (instead of this temporary one).

In AuthCompanion's .env file change "APPLICATION_ORIGIN" to the URL of your application. Example:

APPLICATION_ORIGIN=http://localhost:3002/client/v1/home
